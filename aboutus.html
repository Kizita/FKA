HOW FKA GAS COMPANY LTD WORKS

FKA Gas Company Limited is a Ghanaian owned gas company which has been operarating since 2022 to provide
cleaner and environmentally friendly Liquified Petroleum Gas(LPG) to households and commercial entities across Ghana
FKA Gas aims to make LPG available,accessible and affordable to all Ghanaians.

FKA is the fastest way to have your gas filled and delivered to you conviniently at your your door step.It is all about swapping
your empty gas cylinder with a filled one and off you go.No carrying of gas cylinder on your shoulders over a distance to a Gas
station .You can swap your empty 6kg up to 52kg gas cylinder to a filled one withiin koforidua vicinity.If you dont have an empty
gas cylinder to swap over ,or would need an extra gas bottle on stanby at home, you can contact us for a new one.

The 15kg gas cylinder is the standard and mostly used by many homes and the smalller 6kg gas cylinder is mostly used by students
as well as a backup cylinder
No need to check about the expiry dates of cylinders,they are al checked for you.All FKA gas cylinders are all refurbished and leak tested
and also incorperates the latest valve connections to suit new applaince and many regulator models.
